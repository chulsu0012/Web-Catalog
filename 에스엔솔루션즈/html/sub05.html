Brand

GOGOFARM

‘Gogo Farm’ means to have fun farming,
and aims to solve the labor shortage in
rural areas and automate agriculture.

ROBONGE

‘Robongi’ is a smart farm automated
harvesting robot that detects and
harvests greenhouse crops on its own.

DABEAR

‘Dabear’ is a remote-controlled mowing robot
that can safely mow from a distance,
solving disaster prevention and mowing
manpower problems.
